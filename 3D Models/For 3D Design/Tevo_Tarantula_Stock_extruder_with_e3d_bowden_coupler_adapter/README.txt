# Summary

This extruder is 4mm thicker than the stock extruder, Its much easier to put filament and it uses the e3d coupler.
Printed in Cura
the top was used with adaptive layers so that it would print the top in .1mm and the rest at .2mm
the bottom was printed at just .2mm

this is a remix of B-cup's flexible extruder upgrade, and smallgrans remix
